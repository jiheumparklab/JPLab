About the Lab

The Jiheum Park Lab is dedicated to advancing research at the intersection of computational methods and biological sciences. Our research focuses on developing innovative approaches to understanding complex biological systems through data-driven analysis and cutting-edge computational techniques.

Led by Dr. Jiheum Park, our interdisciplinary team combines expertise in computer science, statistics, and biology to tackle fundamental questions in the life sciences. We welcome collaborations and inquiries from researchers, students, and industry partners.

Research Interests

Research

Current Projects

Deep Learning for Protein Structure Prediction

Developing advanced neural network architectures to predict protein structures from amino acid sequences. Funded by NSF Grant #12345.

Statistical Methods for Single-Cell RNA-seq Analysis

Creating novel statistical approaches for analyzing high-dimensional single-cell genomics data. Collaboration with Stanford Medical School.

Machine Learning for Drug Discovery

Applying machine learning techniques to accelerate the drug discovery process through computational screening and optimization.
